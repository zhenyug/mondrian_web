Got a great idea involving ML/CV technology?

Machine Learning and Computer Vision are important to make your start-up stand out, or to make your traditional business more efficient. We provide an opportunity for you to try out such ideas without making expensive and risky commitment to founding a research and development team by yourself.

Actionable steps towards the ultimate solution with a roadmap.

Integrated Solution

A final product or component in your application or business.


How it works? First send us an email at info@pinocchiotech.com and tell us what you are working on and what you want to do next, briefly. Then we will setup a meeting with you to gather more information to provide you an estimation about: 1. what we can do, 2. how long does it take, 3. how much does it cost.

How much does it cost? It depends... on your project and your requirements. A workable prototype with the core algorithm and code ready usually costs around 10K to 50K.

What if I can't / don't want to pay this amount of cash? We will structure payment as part cash and part equity and/or revenue share.

Want a long term collaboration? If you are a large/stable corporation wants to work with us in the long term, we accept the consultant-in-residence model.
